About My Kai Sourcing

Based in Shenzhen, helping overseas buyers source from China with confidence since 2018.

Your Local Partner in China's Manufacturing Hub

I'm based in Shenzhen — the heart of China's manufacturing and supply chain. With over 8 years of experience in sourcing, quality control, and export logistics, I help overseas buyers find the right suppliers and manage the entire procurement process.

Whether you're a startup looking for your first production run or an established business expanding your supplier base, I work as your on-the-ground eyes and ears — communicating directly with factories in Chinese and English, so you don't have to worry about language barriers or time zone delays.

I believe in transparency, quality, and long-term relationships. Every client gets honest advice, detailed reports, and a dedicated partner who genuinely cares about their success.
