Our Services

End-to-end sourcing solutions tailored to your business needs.

What We Offer

Full-Service Sourcing, Start to Finish

From finding the right factory to shipping the finished product — we handle every step so you can focus on growing your business.

Product Sourcing

We research and identify reliable suppliers across China for any product category. You provide the specs — we find the best match in quality, price, and production capacity. We typically present 2-3 qualified options with a full comparison.

Factory Audit

We visit factories in person to assess capabilities, certifications, production lines, and working conditions. You receive a detailed report with photos and video before making any commitment.

Quality Control

Pre-production, during-production, and final inspection. We check materials, workmanship, dimensions, and functionality — with photo and video evidence at every stage.

Logistics & Shipping

We coordinate the entire shipping process — booking cargo, handling documentation, customs clearance, and last-mile delivery. FOB, CIF, DDP — any incoterm, any destination.

Custom Packaging & Branding

Custom packaging, labels, and branding to meet your market requirements. We work directly with factories to develop packaging that stands out on shelves and online.

Supplier Negotiation

We negotiate pricing, payment terms, and lead times on your behalf. With local knowledge and long-standing relationships, we secure better terms than you'd get reaching out directly.
